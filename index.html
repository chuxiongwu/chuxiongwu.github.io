About

I am a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor in the School of Computing at Southern Illinois University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Computer Science Department at George Mason University, advised by Dr. Qiang Zeng. I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from the University of South Carolina and a B.E. in Electronic Information Engineering from Beihang University.

I am broadly interested in Systems Security, with a focus on cyber-physical systems (CPS) and artificial intelligence (AI) systems.
